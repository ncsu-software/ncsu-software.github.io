# News

- 10/2023, **Dr. Sandeep Kuttal gave a keyonte at SCAM and VISSOFT 2023!**
    - Great job from Dr. Sandeep Kuttal! The talk is titled *IDEs as the Bridge: Connecting Humans and Code* at the IEEE International Working Conference on Source Code Analysis & Manipulation (SCAM) and on Software Visualization (VISSOFT). Bogotá, Colombia, October 2023. [More Details](https://sandeepkuttal.github.io/kuttal/index.html)

- 10/2023, **Our student Leon Shahnewaz (supervised by Dr. Sandeep Kuttal) presented a paper at VLHCC 2023**
    - Congratulations to our students Leon Shahnewaz, Mahzabin Tamanna, and Dr. Sandeep Kuttal! The paper is titled *Comparing Foraging Behavior Across Code Hosting and Q&A Platforms through a Gender Lens* at IEEE Symposium on Visual Languages and Human-Centric Computing (VLHCC). Washington, DC, USA. [More Details](https://sandeepkuttal.github.io/kuttal/index.html)

- 09/2023, **Dr. Tim Menzies was designated ASE Fellow!**
    - Congratulations to Dr. Tim Menzies! He was designated a Fellow of Automated Software Engineering at the 38th IEEE/ACM International Conference on Automated Software Engineering (ASE). [More Details](https://timm.fyi/)

- 09/23, **Dr. Wesley K. G. Assunção Won Best Paper Award at SPLC 2023!**
    - Congratulations to Dr. Wesley K. G. Assunção and his co-authors from the Federal University of Paraná, on recently winning the Best Research Paper award at the 27th ACM International Systems and Software Product Line Conference (SPLC). [More Details](https://wesleyklewerton.github.io/)
